With the Metar information module, you are able to request worldwide weather
information from I C eigh O weather stations provided by the american weather
service N O eigh eigh.

To get a list of pre-defined stations send 0 1, followed by the number sign.

Please ask your local svixlink sysop to add stations of your interest, or
use one of the available search functions.

Send a single number sign to leave the module.
